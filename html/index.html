Animation Playback

Plays a multi-layer 2D rig exported from Unity. Transform tracks + sprite-frame swaps, evaluated against authored keyframes.

Coloring Book

Tap-to-fill regions in line-art pictures. Hybrid label-map approach: each picture is an outline PNG plus a region-id PNG sampled on tap. See html/PLAN.md.

Live

Dino Drop

Matter.js physics on a stegosaurus silhouette. Drag toys from a drawer onto the dino — they slide down the back and tail. Tap the dino to slap them all back into the drawer.

Live

Sound Pads

A 4×4 grid of brightly colored launchpad pads. Tap to play notes from a C major pentatonic scale across two octaves. Web Audio API synth, no library. Multi-touch + keyboard.

Live

Sticker Board

Decorate a blank playmat with a dozen cartoon stickers. Reposition, rotate, and scale individual stickers; the board persists in localStorage between sessions.

Live

Wrecking Crane

Matter.js construction site. Drag the crane's arm to swing a tethered wrecking ball; smash a stack of brick walls, doors, windows, and a chimney into a tumbling cascade. Reset the house and start over.
